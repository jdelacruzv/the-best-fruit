Frozen Mango Pulp

About us

The Best Fruit SAS is a Colombian company dedicated to the commercialization and export of 100% natural fruit pulp. It was created with the sole purpose of meeting the demand and requirement of the international and national market. Currently our products are characterized by high standards of quality, certification and compliance with the standards of the food sector.

We have specialized machinery for fruit processing, a rapid freezing chamber, cold storage rooms, suitable transportation, qualified personnel and an excellent raw material that allows us to maintain a constant supply of products to our clients and always with excellent service. responsibility and quality.

Our customer service team focused on offering the best service, always ready to work hand in hand with the customer to make the service a pleasant experience. We serve all types of clients, from homes to supermarkets, schools and hotels, always with the best quality of the product, speed in the dispatch and excellent service.

Mission

The Best Fruit Company's mission is to export and commercialize fruit pulp processed and packed under the highest quality standards with a qualified human talent, seeking to satisfy the demand of the international market.

Vision

Position ourselves by 2025 as the most recognized company in the international distribution of frozen fruit pulp,through corporate transparency, as well as the credibility and trust of our clients.

Corporate Policies

Quality politics

We work under the premise of customer satisfaction, providing high quality and hygienic products.


For The Best Fruit, the permanent innovation and continuous improvement of its processes allows endless possibilities of adaptation to an increasingly demanding market.


Our clients have confidence in the human team and in the transparent management of our company, based on the high level of training and experience and on compliance with legal and regulatory requirements, generating loyalty ties with clients and suppliers.


We are part of a production and logistics chain where the permanent strengthening of commercial relations is linked to the policy of total satisfaction to the expectations of consumers.

Environmental Policy

We contribute and contribute with the conservation of the biodiversity of nature, implementing responsible actions with the environment.


We abide by and respect the environmental legislation and other applicable regulations established in each of the countries where we have a presence, as well as the relevant conventions, agreements and treaties and the voluntary commitments that The Best Fruit and its allies have contracted.


We identify and assess the phytosanitary risks that the development of our activities may generate on the natural systems and social environments where we are present and implement the necessary measures for their management.


We develop effective education and training processes aimed at strengthening the relationship between our human talent and the environment.

Occupational Health and Safety Policy

We promote the comprehensive self-care of our staff, emphasizing responsibility and commitment on a permanent basis, performing safely during the execution of activities in all workplaces.


We recognize self-care as a personal attitude that favors the development of safe work styles and healthy life habits.


Workers must take care of their health by reporting the dangers and risks to which they are exposed, supplying truthful information and complying with the regulations on Health and Safety at Work.
