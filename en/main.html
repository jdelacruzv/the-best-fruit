Our products

Frozen Mango Pulp

It is a 100% natural product, without preservatives or additives, obtained directly by the extraction of fresh, healthy and ripe fruits, preserving its texture, scent, flavor and nutrients. It is rich in vitamins A, C, E and contains high percentages of magnesium, potassium, fiber and cleansing acids

Among its benefits, it stands out for the stimulation of defenses, the prevention of degenerative diseases, the protection of the skin and mucous membranes. Among its advantages, it stands out how to maintain its flavor to prepare juices, cocktails, smoothies, and desserts.

Frozen fruit pulp can be kept much longer than fruit and has little or no loss of nutrients, unlike pasteurized pulp. Frozen pulp can be kept and used for up to one year.

Frozen Pineapple Pulp

Natural product, not concentrated or fermented, without additives or preservatives, it contains 100% fruit pulp. Obtained by the disintegration and sieving of the edible part of the ripe pineapple. This product is low in sodium, fat and cholesterol free. Contains vitamin A, C and folic acid. It is a good source of potassium, it contains calcium and iron.

Some of its nutrients strengthen the defenses, fight the action of free radicals, acting positively on the thyroid and the nervous system. In addition, it is credited with the benefit that it can reduce the risk of some types of cancer.

Pineapple pulp can be used in the preparation of sauces, ice cream, desserts and juices. The shelf life of this fruit pulp is one year, it is recommended to be in freezing conditions of (-18° C), without interruption of the cold chain.

Blackberry Frozen Pulp

The frozen blackberry pulp is a 100% natural product, it contains vitamins C and A, it is free of seed residues. Weight loss benefits are credited with its low sugar content. Its nutrients help nourish bones, teeth, skin and the generation of collagen.

Due to its high content of vitamin C, it increases defenses against the flu and other infectious diseases. In addition, it favors the proper functioning of the eyes and revitalizes the skin due to its vitamin A content. It is also attributed diuretic properties.

The blackberry is some fruit rich in fiber, which facilitates intestinal transit. It is a healthy and refreshing food.

Lulo Frozen Pulp

Product obtained from pulpy fruits, through an adequate technological process, without preservatives or preservatives, 100% natural. Free of seed residues. It has about 45 calories per 100g of fruit. Contains vitamin C, phosphorus, niacin, iron, calcium, and ascorbic acid.

Its high iron content gives it diuretic and toning properties for the body. Being an important source of phosphorus and vitamin A, it helps in the formation of strong hair, nails and bones. It also helps to improve nervous diseases. Healing properties are attributed to it, as it helps to treat diseases located in the gums and toothache, among many others

All our products meet the requirements of the NTC (Colombian Technical Standard) in terms of physical-chemical and microbiological parameters. The fruit pulps are preserved and stored frozen at a temperature of (-18 ° C).

About us?

The Best Fruit SAS is a Colombian company dedicated to the commercialization and export of 100% natural fruit pulp. It was created with the sole purpose of satisfying the demand and requirement of the international and national market. Currently our products are characterized by high standards of quality, certification and compliance with the standards of the food sector.

We have specialized machinery for fruit processing, a rapid freezing chamber, cold storage rooms, suitable transportation, qualified personnel and excellent raw materials that allow us to maintain a constant supply of products for our clients and always with excellent service. responsibility and quality.

We have a highly qualified work team, focused on offering the best service, always ready to work hand in hand with the client to make the service a pleasant experience. We serve all types of clients, from homes to supermarkets, schools and hotels, always with the best quality of the product, speed of delivery and excellent service.
